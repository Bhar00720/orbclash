How to Play

1

Move & Shoot

P1: WASD + Space to shoot
P2: Arrows + Enter to shoot

2

Dash

P1: Shift · P2: Ctrl — Quick burst of speed to dodge bullets. 3-second cooldown.

3

Charged Shots

Hold Q (P1) or 0 (P2) to charge. Bigger, faster, more damage!

4

Powerups

🛡️ Shield · ⚡ Speed · 🔱 Triple Shot · 💚 Heal

5

Win Condition

First to 3 round wins takes the match. Bullets bounce off walls!
